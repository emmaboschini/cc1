FLOOD DATA.
RAW & UNFILTERED.

This platform serves as a comprehensive digital repository for major global flood events documented since 1985. By leveraging high-resolution satellite imagery, orbital radar sensors, and multi-modal instrumental records, we track the temporal evolution, spatial extent, and socio-economic impact of flooding worldwide. Our mission is to provide researchers, urban planners, and environmental scientists with verified, high-fidelity geospatial data to better understand historical flood patterns and improve future resilience strategies.

Methodologies

Data is collected via a multi-modal approach combining **MODIS and Landsat satellite imagery**, orbital radar sensors, and ground-based instrumental records. By integrating high-resolution spectral bands with synthetic aperture radar (SAR), the archive captures flood extents even through dense cloud cover and nocturnal cycles. This geospatial raw data is supplemented by cross-referenced governmental reports and international news archives to ensure high-fidelity spatial and casualty metrics across every documented event.
